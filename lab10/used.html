Examples of Social Networks that use ActivityPub

  • Mastodon – Microblogging, similar to Twitter/X.
  • PeerTube – Decentralized video hosting, alternative to YouTube.
  • Pixelfed – Photo sharing, similar to Instagram.
  • Diaspora – One of the earliest federated social networks.
  • NodeBB – Next generation community forum software
  • Lemmy – A forum and link aggregator for the fediverse.
  • WriteFreely – An open source platform for building a writing space on the web.
  • Friendica – A Decentralized Social Network, similar to Facebook.
  • Hubzilla – Open source privacy-respecting all-in-one home base for collaboration and social interactions.
  • Funkwhale – A platform for all your audio.
  • Bookwyrm – Social Reading and Reviewing, Decentralized.
  • Owncast – Free and open source live video and web chat.
  • Pinetta – ActivityPub-enabled pinboard thingy, in the style of Pinterest.
  • Nextcloud – Remote collaboration made easy.
