About Me

I have a sarcastic nature and I’m not a fan of authority. Most mundane and boring things, aside from my morning cup of coffee, don’t appeal to me. I also strongly dislike anything that feels fake or forced. So, what do I actually enjoy? I appreciate effort. It’s all too easy to be lazy, but nothing great comes from that. If something comes easily to you, consider trying something else, because easy often leads to boredom. It’s the friction in life that makes you stronger, not comfort.
